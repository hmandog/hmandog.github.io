Warriors of Antiquity

*Warriors of Antiquity* is a collectible card game where players lead ancient civilizations in epic battles for supremacy. Choose from factions like Rome, Egypt, and Dynastic China, each with unique strategies, units, and abilities to conquer cities and dominate the battlefield.

Game Features

  • Factions with Unique Playstyles: Command armies with distinct strengths, from Rome's powerful infantry to Egypt's swift cavalry and China's tactical ranged units.
  • City Management: Capture and control cities to produce resources, enhance your strategy, and unlock powerful bonuses.
  • Resource Strategy: Balance gold and food to deploy units, build armies, and outmaneuver opponents.
  • Dynamic Cards: Use event and tactic cards to surprise enemies, disrupt plans, or bolster your forces.

Factions

  • Romans: Excel in disciplined infantry with balanced attack and defense, ideal for sustained battles.
  • Egyptians: Harness the speed of cavalry and chariots for lightning-fast strikes and flanking maneuvers.
  • Dynastic China: Command ranged units with powerful abilities to control the battlefield from a distance.

Deck Composition

Each faction's deck includes:

  • Units: 12 units (8 Common, 3 Uncommon, 1 Rare).
  • Tactics: 3 faction-specific tactic cards to shift the tide of battle.
  • Events: 3 event cards to create unexpected twists.
  • Cities: 2 city cards representing the heart of your empire.

Neutral Cities

Art and Components

*Warriors of Antiquity* features elegant artwork inspired by the styles of ancient civilizations, with vibrant yet historically grounded designs. The game includes faction decks, city cards, and booster packs for expanded play.
