Warriors of Antiquity

*Warriors of Antiquity* is a collectible card game set in the ancient world, where players command armies of unique units from various factions, including Romans, Egyptians, and Dynastic China. The game involves tactical strategies, resource management, and engaging combat.

Game Features

  • Faction Diversity: Players can choose from multiple factions, each with unique units and abilities.
  • Resource Management: Players utilize gold and food to deploy units and maintain control of cities.
  • City Control: Capture and control cities to gain resources and strategic advantages.
  • Unique Units: Each faction has specific units with distinct strengths and weaknesses.
  • Event and Tactic Cards: Players can use special cards to influence gameplay with buffs and resource generation.

Factions

  • Romans: Focused on infantry units with balanced attack and defense.
  • Egyptians: Specialize in cavalry and chariot units for rapid strikes.
  • Dynastic China: Excels in ranged units, featuring crossbowmen and infantry for tactical superiority.

Card Structure

Each faction's deck includes:

  • Units: 12 units per faction (4 Common, 3 Uncommon, 2 Rare, 1 Epic).
  • Tactics: 3 unique tactics per faction.
  • Events: 3 event cards per faction.
  • Cities: 2 city cards per faction.

Neutral Cities

  • Rome: Major City - 3 Gold, 1 Food - Defense: 6 - Grants +1 attack to ranged units.
  • Thebes: Major City - 3 Gold, 2 Food - Defense: 5 - Draw an extra card when deploying units.
  • Xi'an: Minor City - 2 Gold, 1 Food - Defense: 3 - +1 defense bonus to melee units.
  • Memphis: Minor City - 2 Gold, 1 Food - Defense: 4 - Grants +1 attack to cavalry units.
  • Luoyang: Major City - 4 Gold - Defense: 7 - Gain an additional Gold resource when captured.
  • Chang'an: Minor City - 1 Gold, 2 Food - Defense: 4 - Trade 1 Food for 1 Gold once per turn.

Game Art and Components

The artwork for the cards and booster packs features minimalist, vintage-style illustrations with earthy tones and simplified shapes to enhance the game's thematic experience.
