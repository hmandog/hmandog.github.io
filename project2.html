Handheld Computer

Handheld computer is a fancy way of saying calculator but I plan on creating a touchscreen calculator using an Arduino Mega and Nextion NX4827P043-011C HMI display. The project will be my largest and most complex so far.

Features

  • Basic Mode: Simple arithmetic operations.
  • Scientific Mode: Functions like sin, cos, log, exponents (up to Â±12).
  • Engineering Mode: A menu with predetermined formulas where users provide inputs after selecting the formula.
  • Custom Formula Library: Storage for user-defined formulas.
  • Unit Converter: Conversions between units (length, weight, temperature, etc.).
  • Data Logging: Documenting calculations and their answers.
  • Battery Indicator: An indicator for battery life on the display.
