Educational Circuit Board

This educational trainer circuit board is designed to teach the fundamentals of electronics through hands-on modules. It features key circuits such as series and parallel resistors, voltage dividers, RC circuits with LED flashers, logic gates, basic sensors, transistor switching, signal generation, and power supply regulation. The board is powered by either a USB-C or barrel jack input and includes regulated 5V and 3.3V outputs, making it a versatile tool for beginners to explore both analog and digital concepts in a structured, practical way.
