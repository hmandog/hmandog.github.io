About Me

Hi, I’m Hayden Hickerson, and welcome to my website! I’m a tech enthusiast and maker who loves working on electronics and robotics projects. This site is where I document my journey, share my projects, and hopefully inspire others who share my passion.

Ever since I built my first project, I’ve been hooked on learning how things work. From basic electronics to complex robotics, each new challenge presents an opportunity to learn something new and push the limits of what I can create.

My Mission

My mission is to continuously explore new technologies, create innovative projects, and share the knowledge I’ve gained with others. Whether it’s building a robot or designing a handheld computer, I aim to inspire others to pursue their own creative endeavors.
