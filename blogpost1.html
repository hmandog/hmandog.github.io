Welcome to My Website!

Published on: October 2, 2024

Hi, I'm Hayden Hickerson, and I’m excited to welcome you to my personal website. This site is a platform where I’ll showcase my projects, share my journey into the world of electronics and robotics, and offer useful resources to fellow creators and tech enthusiasts.

Why I Created This Website

The purpose of this website is simple: to create a central hub for my projects. Over the years, I’ve worked on various electronics and robotic builds, and I wanted a place where I could document them in detail, share schematics and files, and provide insights that might help others working on similar projects. This website also serves as a growing portfolio of my work.

What You'll Find Here

In addition to detailed breakdowns of my projects, you’ll find blog posts where I discuss my experiences, lessons learned, and tips for overcoming common challenges in electronics and robotics. There’s also a resources page where I’ll link to useful guides, datasheets, and websites that have helped me along the way.

A Bit About Me

I’ve always been fascinated by how things work, and my passion for electronics and robotics has grown over time. Whether it's building a robot from scratch or programming a handheld computer, I love combining problem-solving with creativity. I hope this website can inspire others to dive into their own projects and bring ideas to life!

Thanks for stopping by, and I hope you find my work and insights helpful. Stay tuned for more updates!
